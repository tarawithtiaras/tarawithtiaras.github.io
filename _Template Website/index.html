  • Hello!

    I'm a developer, quality specialist, and a DevOps advocate.

    I like to tell people that I didn't find QA. It found me.

    Having spent nearly a decade as a Manual Tester and Automation Developer writing test cases and delivering automated results for clients, I recently moved into the community side of DevOps when I found myself in the Salesforce space. I've been blessed enough to find a great community in the tech world and love to give back as much as I can through hosting webinars, writing, and speaking at conferences.

    My automations have mainly been frameworks of my own design in C# and other backend languages, but I have been known to spend some quality time on the UI side of test automation.
